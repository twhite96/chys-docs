Frustrated by Grammarly and Hemingway apps' inability to process markdown blog posts, I decided to make my own site to process markdown blog posts or other texts. I settled on [Materialize.css](http://materializecss.com/), a flexible framework for [Material Design Lite](https://getmdl.io/index.html)for the UI because I like colorful things.
